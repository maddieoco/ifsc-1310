About Me

I'm a post-bacc student at UALR this year in Web Design and Development. My background is in fine art; I have a bachelor's degree in Fiber Arts & Textiles from the Kansas City Art Institute. I grew up in North Little Rock and graduated from NLRHS in 2014.

After I graduated from college with my shiny and hard-won art degree, I stayed in Kansas City for a couple of years, working odd jobs and living with friends. Predictably, this kind of sucked. A career change was proving itself to be extremely necessary.
